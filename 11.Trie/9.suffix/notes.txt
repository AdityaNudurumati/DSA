==================================================
TRIE / 9. SUFFIX TRIE
==================================================

A trie of ALL suffixes of a string. Answers substring queries (a substring is a prefix
of some suffix). Rare in interviews due to O(n^2) memory; suffix arrays/automata scale
better.

Sub-patterns
│
├── Substring Search      query = does this substring exist? (prefix of a suffix)
├── Repeated Substrings     deepest node with >= 2 distinct continuations
└── Pattern Queries          many substring membership checks

--------------------------------------------------
Idea
--------------------------------------------------
Insert s[i:] for every i. A substring exists iff it is a path from the root.
Longest repeated substring = deepest internal node reached by >= 2 suffixes.
