GREEDY / 12. ADVANCED GREEDY
==================================================

Greedy combined with another technique.

Sub-patterns
│
├── Sweep Line             sort events, +1/-1, scan (skyline, overlaps)
├── Greedy + Binary Search   binary search the answer; a greedy check tests feasibility
├── Greedy + DSU             greedily union under a sorted order (Kruskal-flavored)
└── Greedy Proof Problems     where the hard part is proving the greedy is correct
