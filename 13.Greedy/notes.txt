--------------------------------------------------
The greedy mindset
--------------------------------------------------
1. Find a LOCAL rule (sort by end time, take the most frequent, pick the cheapest).
2. Argue it's SAFE: an exchange argument shows swapping toward the greedy choice never
   makes the answer worse. If you can't argue it, suspect DP instead.
Common sorts: by end time (intervals), by ratio (fractional knapsack), by deadline.
