==================================================
LINKED LIST / 7. LIST REARRANGEMENT
==================================================

When to think of this?
- reorder nodes by position parity, halves, or rotation
- often combines find-middle + reverse + merge

Sub-patterns
│
├── Odd Even List    group odd-indexed then even-indexed nodes
├── Reorder List      L0->Ln->L1->Ln-1->... (middle + reverse + merge)
├── Rotate List        rotate right by k (make circular, then cut)
└── Partition List      nodes < x before nodes >= x, order preserved

--------------------------------------------------
Template — reorder list
--------------------------------------------------
# 1) find middle (fast-slow)
# 2) reverse the second half
# 3) merge the two halves alternately
