--------------------------------------------------
Core idea
--------------------------------------------------
A hash map/set trades O(n) space for O(1) average lookup/insert. Reach for it when
you need: counts, "have I seen X", complements (target - x), grouping by a key,
prefix bookkeeping, or O(1) membership. Python: dict, collections.Counter,
collections.defaultdict, set, collections.OrderedDict.
