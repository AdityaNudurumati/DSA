11. ADVANCED (the remaining ~10-20%)

The core 10 patterns cover the bulk of array interviews. This folder holds the
advanced layer — less frequent, but high-signal at product companies. Each concept
is a sub-folder with its own notes.txt + solved problems.

Sub-patterns   (each maps to a sub-folder; see its notes.txt for the checklist)
│
├── 1.heap/             k-th / top-k / median / merge-k via priority queue
├── 2.bit_manipulation/ XOR tricks, single/missing number, bit counting
├── 3.divide_conquer/   merge-sort counting (inversions, reverse pairs), majority
├── 4.sorting_tricks/   custom comparators / derived sort keys
├── 5.math/             rotate, sieve, cycle detection, number theory
├── 6.union_find/       disjoint-set connectivity (graph-flavored)
└── 7.trie/             prefix tree + bitwise trie (string/XOR-flavored)
