==================================================
ADVANCED — 5. MATH / NUMBER THEORY
==================================================

A grab-bag where a numeric insight (a formula, a sieve, a reversal identity, cycle
detection) replaces brute force.

When to think of this?
- digit manipulation, primality, divisibility
- in-place rearrangement via index arithmetic
- "does this sequence terminate / cycle"

--------------------------------------------------
Tricks
--------------------------------------------------
- Rotate by k:   reverse all, reverse first k, reverse rest.
- Sieve:         mark multiples from i*i; outer loop to sqrt(n).
- Cycle detect:  a set, or Floyd's tortoise & hare for O(1) space.
