Sub-patterns
│
├── Left Shift           n << k  multiplies by 2^k
├── Right Shift           n >> k  divides by 2^k (floor for non-negatives)
├── Multiply/Divide by 2   shift instead of arithmetic
└── Binary Conversion       to/from base-2 strings
